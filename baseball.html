Description

The Baseball Performance Mentorship is built for high school baseball players looking to move on to the next level. Develop strength, agility, and speed during our gym training groups built around each athletesâ€™ individual body and needs.

Chalk Talks
Chalk Talks are times during our training sessions when we discuss the habits and mental skills of high performing ballplayers competing in the college, MiLB, and MLB ranks. Athletes thinking critically about off the field topics that affect on the field performance: discipline, self-talk, routines, stress, and others. Each Talk is actionable and meant to be practiced by the athlete immediately.

Each athlete must go through a 1:1 assessment with coach Kyle where biometric and performance data is taken. Kyle and the athlete will also set actionable performance habits designed to achieve their goals. At the end of the summer there will be an exit assessment to measure progress and plan for the off-season.
