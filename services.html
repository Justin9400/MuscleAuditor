Our Process

We like to start with a one on one, in-person consultation in order to make sure that what the Muscle Auditor provides is the right fit for you, as well as to answer every question or concern you might have. This is a vital step in ensuring both parties have similar expectations and will enhance every step of your experience. After the initial consultation, our process involves four key stages: Audit, Report, Train, and Progress.

Audit

This is a 1-2 hour assessment of your body's current state of muscular control and stability. The goal of the assessment is to take in data of your standing and supine posture along with your active range of motion so that we can look back and determine whether or not we're going in the right direction. Reassessments occur every 1-3 months.

    Our 1:1 sessions offer customized fitness training designed around your unique body structure, needs, and goals. Whether you're an athlete, a full time employee, a parent who is tired of their constant pain and irritation, or someone who needs help to slow down the aging process, Kyle creates a unique, individualized exercise experience just for you!
